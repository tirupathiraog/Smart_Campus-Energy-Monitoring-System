About Smart Campus Energy Monitoring System

A web-based energy monitoring platform developed to monitor electricity consumption across various buildings, detect abnormal energy usage, generate alerts, and provide real-time analytics for efficient campus energy management.

← Back to Dashboard

Project Overview

The Smart Campus Energy Monitoring System continuously monitors energy consumption in different various buildings. It stores energy readings, analyzes power usage, detects abnormal consumption, generates alerts, and presents all information through an interactive dashboard. The system helps administrators reduce energy wastage and improve overall operational efficiency.

Objective

Monitor campus energy usage in real time and detect abnormal power consumption automatically.

Vision

Build an intelligent and sustainable campus by providing accurate real-time energy monitoring and analytics.

Mission

Help institutions optimize electricity usage through smart monitoring, early alert generation, and efficient reporting.

Key Features

Live Dashboard

Real-time energy monitoring dashboard.

High Usage Detection

Detects abnormal power consumption automatically.

Alert Notifications

Instant popup alerts with acknowledgement support.

Building Monitoring

Track energy consumption building-wise.

Analytics

Interactive energy trend visualization using Chart.js.

Excel Reports

Download energy readings and alerts in Excel format.
